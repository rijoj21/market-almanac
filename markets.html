Five markets,

Why these markets matter for index funds

An index fund doesn't pick winners — it buys a whole market in one go. So the market you choose decides what you own. A US index is mostly technology; a European one leans on pharma and luxury; an Indian one is heavy with banks. A "whole world" fund quietly blends them all by size.

Diversification
Spreading across regions means no single country's bad year sinks everything.
Different engines
Tech, medicine and banking rise and fall at different times — together they smooth the ride.
Know what you own
"Global" funds are mostly US by value. Seeing the weights is the point of this guide.
